Mark Boas

One half of the team that created the popular media library jPlayer, Mark occupies himself with web media, real-time communications and data visualisations. He lectures on Interactive Media for Masters students at the University of Florence and was a 2012 Knight-Mozilla Open News fellow for Al Jazeera English.

Mark is currently attempting to take media somewhere different with his Hyperaudio initiative.

Nicola Hughes

Nicola is a journalist turned coder. She is a tinkerer of things, a lover of animals and a digger of data. She is a physicist, zoologist, anthropologist, journalist developer. She scrapes sites, forms databases, builds tools and tell stories.

Having left CNN International to learn to code with tech startup ScraperWiki, she became a Knight-Mozilla OpenNews fellow at the Guardian in 2012.

She now codes in The Times of London newsroom.
